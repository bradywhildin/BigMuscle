About Big Muscle

Big Muscle is a website that brings together fitness-oriented individuals.

The articles section displays fitness-related articles posted by users. The articles range from personal fitness stories to tips for a more successful fitness experience.

The pictures section allows users to upload pictures. The pictures can be anything related to fitness, from healthy meals they cooked up to progress pictures.

Use the nav bar to navigate to the various sections. To make full use of the website, create an account to allow for creating articles and adding pictures.
